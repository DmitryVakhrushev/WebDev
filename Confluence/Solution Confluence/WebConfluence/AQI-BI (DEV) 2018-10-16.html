Assessment for Quality Improvement (AQI)

Welcome to the world of AQI Business Intelligence!

The following BI dashboards will provide you with an overview of the status of the assessment progress as well as the visits and quality ratings.

This information can also be queried as far back as 2008.

However, there are some things that you need to keep in mind when reviewing the data:

  • The assessment measure has changed over the years
  • The staff who administer the measure have changed
  • Different levels of data was stored over the years

So what does this mean when analyzing the data? Be mindful when comparing year over year data.

For example, it would not be fair to compare 2008 to 2014, as the assessment measure has changed.

It does make sense to compare 2014 to 2015, as all variables are equal.

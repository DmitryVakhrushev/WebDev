Data Quality Issues from Waitlist system has been identified. These issues are categorized as Date Issues and Enrolment Status Issues.

Date Issues

  • Required Start Date before DOB or Enrolment Effective Date
  • Children with Future Enrolment Date
  • Active Overage Children
  • Source Table Date Quality Issues


Enrolment Status Issues

  • Pending (PEND) or Waiting for Formal Care (WTFC) Status
  • WTAD or ALLC but still placed
  • WTTR or WTFS status but are not placed
  • Committed and Enrolled but not Placed
  • WDWL or WITHDR but still placed
  • Multiple Statuses
  • No Current Enrolment Status
